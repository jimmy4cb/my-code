About us

People-powered eco-adventure and wellness movement

Lakeside Adventure Community is a community-based eco-tourism group in Kisumu that brings together hikers, cyclists, campers, photographers, and culture lovers for meaningful outdoor experiences around Lake Victoria and beyond.

Core activities

Fun adventures for every pace

Guided hikes & cycling

Fitness-building hikes and scenic cycling trips with local guides, including the popular Korando trail and lakeside routes.

Sip & Paint picnics

Relaxed lakeside gatherings with painting, drinks, photography, and community connection at places like Dunga Hill Camp.

Lake & cultural tours

Boat rides, hippo viewing, Ndere Island excursions, and immersive tours that highlight local heritage and nature.
